You have four options on the main screen.

1. The Learn button, here you can listen to times tables. Press the blue button to pick the times table you want to hear.

2. The Test button, pick the times table (like before) then listen to the sum and type in your answer. Press finish at the end and you'll see how you've done.

3. The Results button shows you your test high scores.

4. The Male / Female buttons changes the voice used.
